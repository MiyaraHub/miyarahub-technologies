FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Almanox.

Is Almanox a subscription?▼
No. Almanox is a one-time purchase at $12.99. No subscription, no ads, no paywalled updates. Buy it once and own it on the App Store or Google Play.
Does it work without a connection?▼
Yes. The full astronomy engine - sun, moon, twilight, Milky Way, dark-sky windows, and the shot planner - runs entirely on your device. Only live weather needs a connection, and everything else keeps working without one.
What is free, and what is Pro?▼
Free gives you the full sun and moon almanac - sunrise and sunset, the golden and blue hours, the twilight ladder, moon phase - plus the Tonight verdict score, one saved location, and every theme in any color. Pro ($12.99, one time) unlocks the Milky Way and dark-sky windows, live weather and sky conditions, ISS passes, the sun and moon shot planner, golden-hour and dark-sky alerts, unlimited saved locations.
Which devices does it run on?▼
iPhone, iPad, and Android, from one beautifully consistent design. It adapts to light, dark, and true-black AMOLED, in any accent color you want - curated shades or your own custom pick.
Do I need an account?▼
No. There is no sign-up, no email, and no MiyaraHub login. Download the app and start planning. Your location is used only to compute your sky and is never sold, shared, or tracked.
How is the Tonight score calculated?▼
The verdict fuses three things into one 0-100 score: how high the Milky Way core climbs, how many hours of true astronomical darkness you get, and how much the moon interferes. It is a fast, honest read on whether tonight is worth heading out.
